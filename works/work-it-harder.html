harder, better, faster, stronger is a music video made almost exclusively with Processing 3 (sans the typography and some minor color adjustments), in which I explored generative art processes in an attempt to coordenate movement, shape and color changes in accordance with changes in beat and general sound intensity.

This work was made for the sake of a design class in my 3rd year in univeristy (May 2017).

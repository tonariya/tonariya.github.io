ABOUT WORKS RESUME

My name is Beatriz, born and raised in a small town in northern Portugal. It was initially through videogames that, from a very young age, I'd come to realize the incredible power of experiences. Specifically: experiences in digital form.

I set out to study Design & Multimedia at the University of Coimbra where I obtained new tools and further explored mediums through which I could satisfy this desire. I learned of something called UI & UX design and how carefully crafted web pages and applications are able to generate engaging digital experiences. Then, by my senior year, I'd finally realize that that was something I very much enjoy doing.

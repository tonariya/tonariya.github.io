ABOUT WORKS RESUME

Harder, better, faster, stronger (2017) is a Daft Punk inspired (hence its namesake) music video, through which I explore generative art processes in an attempt to coordenate movement, shape and color along with beat, rythm and general sound intensity.

This work was made almost exclusively in Processing 3 (with typography and post-production being done in Sony's Vegas Pro) for a college assignment in my 3rd year in univeristy.
